Cats with Bats (alpha)

Long, detailed documentation is a gold mine.


But it's hard to read and find useful information quickly.


When following a long document from top to bottom (for example, a Standard Operating Procedures), people make mistakes.


Also, often people come to others with questions that can be answered by reading the document?


Often, it's faster to just show people directly, 1-on-1.


But that takes resources and time out of your day.


Learning something new is overwhelming.


From long coding tutorials to onboard documents and SOPs.


Replace them with step-by-step, guided learning, and instant Q&A.


Cats with Bats

Cats with Bats solves this by turning your internal training docs or external user guides into micro-learning AI Agents that can guide the reader step-by-step and answer their questions on the fly for quick referencing.


Make sure whoever your audience is gets the most out of your documentation and actually understand them.
