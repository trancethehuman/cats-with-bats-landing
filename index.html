Cats with Bats (alpha)

Remove errors when reading guide docs & SOPs with AI


People following long guide docs and SOPs (Standard Operating Procedures) are bound to make mistakes.


Sometimes they need to ask questions that can be answered right there in the doc.


Sometimes it feels like it's faster to just show them directly, 1-on-1.


But that takes resources and time.


This goes for:

  • Onboarding new employees
  • Coding tutorials
  • Machinery operation steps
  • Security & compliance procedures
  • Learning a new cooking recipe
  • Homework assignments
  • And more.

Some people just want to write their SOPs and guides once and move on; you're passionate about the experience of reading your work.


Why don't you give your employees / new hires / students the best SOP reading experience possible?


Cats with Bats

Cats with Bats solves this by turning your training docs into micro-learning AI Guides that guide the reader step-by-step and answer their questions.


And there are a few other things..
