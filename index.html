Cats with Bats (alpha)

Long, detailed documentation is a gold mine to learn new things.


From "Getting Started" guide to new employee onboarding document.


But if someone is slow at reading, it's difficult to read and avoid making mistakes.


It's also cognitively taxing to find information quickly (imagine a 25-page document).


The biggest issue with docs is how often people come to others with questions that can be answered by reading the document.


Learning something new can be overwhelming.


But not when you're being guided.


From long coding tutorials to onboard documents and SOPs.


Replace them with step-by-step, guided learning, and instant Q&A.


Cats with Bats

Cats with Bats solves this by turning a guide document into micro-learning AI Agents that can guide the reader step-by-step and answer questions instantly.


Don't just write guides. Teach, with AI.


Sign up to be an alpha tester here.


Use cases

  • SOP (Standard Operating Procedures).
  • Walkthroughs, workflow, tutorials for external users of your product.
  • Reading aid for complex new topics or dyslexia.
  • Onboarding document or job aids.
  • Developer Relations tutorials
  • Cooking recipes
