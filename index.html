Long documentation.


People following long guide docs and SOPs (Standard Operating Procedures) are bound to make mistakes.


Often they ask questions that can be answered by the doc.


Often, the document fails to convey meaning and it's faster to just show people directly, 1-on-1.


But that takes resources and time.


Learning something new is overwhelming.


What if you can turn your long docs into AI guides.


From long coding tutorials to onboard documents.


Replace them with step-by-step, guided learning, and instant Q&A.


Cats with Bats

Cats with Bats solves this by turning your internal training docs or external user guides into micro-learning AI Agents that can guide the reader step-by-step and answer their questions.
