Turn any training document into a live AI guide. Reduce misunderstanding and increase engagement in your team training.

Please sign up to be a tester here.

Bring 1-on-1 training to large groups

Company training is often long documents and corny videos. Worse, if you have a question about anything, you'd have to wait until the end of a video or document, or re-read the whole thing to try and find the answer.

  • You want to ask questions as they come up, not wait.
  • You want to be guided, not info-dumped.
  • You want to learn your way, not based on the delivery style of the video or document

Cats with Bats

Cats with Bats solves that by giving the organizer a quick way to convert any existing documents into a teacher, and giving the students access to that teacher. This teacher adapts to the student's learning style and guides them step-by-step through a training course.
