Cats with Bats (alpha)

Create AI-enabled team training & workshop just by writing docs.

Bring 1-on-1 training to large groups

What's the best thing about having a human training another? Learner can ask questions, teacher can set the pace and quiz to solidify learnings.

But that takes resources and time.

Same goes for:

  • Onboarding new employees
  • Showing software engineers how to use a new library
  • Training technicians on how to operate a new machine
  • Training security & compliance
  • Learning a new cooking recipe
  • University / college / high school homework assignments
  • And more.

You might spend weeks or months building a training program using complicated instructional design software, making slides and creating interactive activities.


Or you can just write a "magical" document.


Cats with Bats

Cats with Bats solves this by turning your training docs into micro-learning AI coaches that guide the learner step-by-step and answer their questions.
