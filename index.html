Bring 1-on-1 training to large groups

Company training is often long documents and corny videos. If I have a question about the training, I'd have to wait until the end, or re-read the whole thing to get it. It's 2023 and I don't want that.

  • I want to ask my questions as they come up, not wait.
  • I want to be guided, not info-dumped.
  • I want to learn my way, not either document or video

Cats with Bats

Cats with Bats solves that by giving the organizer a quick way to convert any existing documents into a teacher, and giving me the teacher.
